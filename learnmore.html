Active Living

Staying active is a great way to maintain a healthy lifestyle! Here are some ways to stay active.
- Go for walks outdoors
- Join a community walking or exercising group

Diet

Here are some dietary recommendations from the Heart and Stroke Foundation to improve blood pressure!
- Reduce salt intake! (less than 1 teaspoon a day)
- Fill half your plate with fruits and vegetables at each meal
- Drink lots of water
- Eat fresh foods (not processed) as often as possible
